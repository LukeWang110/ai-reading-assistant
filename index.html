Privacy Policy

AI Reading Assistant Chrome Extension  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser or through services you already use.

1. What the Extension Does

AI Reading Assistant is a Chrome extension that lets you select text on any webpage and receive an instant translation or AI-powered explanation without leaving the page.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no data. The developer has no server, database, or analytics system. Nothing you do with this extension is ever transmitted to us.

3. How Your Selected Text Is Used

  • Quick translation — When you select text, it is sent to the MyMemory Translation API (a public, free translation service) to retrieve a translation. MyMemory's own privacy and usage policy applies to these requests.
  • Ask Claude / Ask ChatGPT — When you click these buttons, a pre-written prompt containing your selected text is stored temporarily in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local) and is used to auto-fill the input box on claude.ai or chatgpt.com. The prompt is deleted from local storage immediately after use. Your interactions with Claude or ChatGPT are governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy and OpenAI's Privacy Policy respectively.

4. Permissions Explained

  • Read and change all your data on websites you visit — Required to detect text selections and display the translation tooltip on any webpage.
  • Storage — Used only to temporarily hold your prompt text while the Claude/ChatGPT popup window loads. No browsing history or personal data is stored.
  • Windows / Tabs — Used to open the Claude or ChatGPT popup window.

5. Third-Party Services

  • MyMemory Translation API — for instant translations.
  • Anthropic Claude — optional, on user request.
  • OpenAI ChatGPT — optional, on user request.

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any of these services.

6. Children's Privacy

This extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the "Last updated" date above will change. Continued use of the extension after an update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
