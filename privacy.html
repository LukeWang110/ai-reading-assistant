Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

AI Reading Assistant – Translate & Explain ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that helps users understand English content while browsing the web by providing instant translations, dictionary definitions, pronunciation, and optional AI-powered explanations.

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension processes and how it is used.

Information We Process

The Extension only processes information that you explicitly choose to use.

When you select a word, sentence, or paragraph, the selected text may be processed to provide:

  • Machine translation
  • Dictionary definitions
  • Pronunciation
  • AI-powered explanations, only when you request them

The Extension never scans webpages automatically and never collects webpage content without your interaction.

Local Storage

The Extension stores a limited amount of information locally using Chrome Storage, including:

  • Today's looked-up words
  • Vocabulary history
  • Extension preferences
  • A temporary prompt used to open ChatGPT or Claude after you click an Explain button

All of this information remains on your device. We do not operate our own servers and do not upload this information.

Third-Party Services

To provide requested functionality, selected text may be transmitted to one or more of the following services:

  • Google Translate
  • MyMemory Translation API
  • DictionaryAPI.dev
  • ChatGPT, only after clicking "Explain with ChatGPT"
  • Claude, only after clicking "Explain with Claude"

These third-party services process your selected text according to their own privacy policies.

We do not control how these third-party services handle information after it is transmitted. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for additional information.

Information We Do Not Collect

The Extension does not collect or store:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Authentication credentials
  • Browsing history
  • Cookies
  • Location information
  • Personally identifiable information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

Selected text is transmitted only to the third-party service necessary to provide the functionality explicitly requested by the user.

Security

Whenever possible, communications with third-party services are transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections.

Your Control

The Extension only processes text that you intentionally select.

You may clear your locally stored vocabulary history at any time from within the Extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated effective date.
