Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

AI Reading Assistant – Translate & Explain ("the Extension") helps users understand English content while browsing the web by providing instant translations, dictionary definitions, pronunciation, and optional AI-powered explanations.

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Extension processes and how it is used.

Information We Process

The Extension only processes content that you explicitly choose. When you select a word, sentence, or paragraph, the selected text may be processed to provide:

  • Machine translation
  • Dictionary definitions
  • Pronunciation
  • AI-powered explanations, only when requested

The Extension does not automatically scan webpages or collect webpage content without your interaction.

Local Storage

The Extension stores limited information locally using Chrome Storage, including:

  • Today's looked-up words
  • Vocabulary history
  • Extension preferences
  • A temporary pending prompt used to open ChatGPT or Claude after you click an Explain button

This information remains on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

Third-Party Services

To provide requested features, selected text may be transmitted to one or more of the following services:

  • Google Translate
  • MyMemory Translation API
  • DictionaryAPI.dev
  • ChatGPT, only after clicking "Explain with ChatGPT"
  • Claude, only after clicking "Explain with Claude"

These third-party services process your selected text according to their own privacy policies.

Information We Do Not Collect

The Extension does not collect or store:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Authentication credentials
  • Browsing history
  • Cookies
  • Location information
  • Personally identifiable information

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with advertisers or data brokers.

Selected text is transmitted only when necessary to provide the functionality requested by the user.

Security

Whenever possible, communications with third-party services are transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections.

Your Control

The Extension only processes text that you intentionally select. You may clear your locally stored vocabulary history from within the Extension.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
